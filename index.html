Project Overview

GrumpyChef is a web/mobile application designed to simplify meal planning and grocery shopping. The platform allows users to inventory ingredients in their homes, create and share recipes, and generate shopping lists based on missing ingredients. With multi-user support, families, roommates, or friends can collaborate on meal planning and grocery shopping effortlessly.
